About Kodiak

Whole-system work.

I'm Kodiak, an integrative therapist and coach in San Diego, and the founder of Pocket of Humanity. I came to this work the long way. Personal training, then holistic nutrition, then clinical counseling — each time realizing the last piece wasn't the whole picture.

How you feel, how you think, how you live, who you're around: it's all one system. That's how I work with people one-on-one, and it's why I built a community around the same idea.
