## Design Direction
Carl Rogers meets Apple. Timeless, world-class, modern but 
deeply warm and human. Not a sales page — a place to feel seen.

Photography: film aesthetic. Slightly warm, slightly muted, 
candid. Natural light. Coastal San Diego. Human moments. 
Earth, water, trees, sky. Personal photos in /images folder.

Typography: humanistic serif for headlines (Lora from Google 
Fonts). Clean humanist sans-serif for body (DM Sans from 
Google Fonts). Large, generous sizing throughout.

Layout: maximum whitespace. Full-bleed imagery. Sections 
breathe. No card-heavy grids. Content sits in open space. 
Rounded corners on buttons and cards — nothing sharp or 
corporate.

Color: warm white/cream background (#FAFAF7). Near-black 
charcoal text (#1C1C1A). One warm accent used sparingly 
for CTAs.

Animations: subtle fade-in on scroll only. Nothing flashy.

## DO NOT
- Use stock photos
- Use gradients
- Use purple, blue, or generic wellness color schemes
- Use Inter, Roboto, or Arial fonts
- Use heavy drop shadows or borders
- Over-animate
- Write cliche wellness copy ("transform your life", 
  "your journey starts here")
